Wombat's Tribute Page

The quadrupedal marsupials who leave cubic feces

Here's a time line of Wombat's history:

  • 1798 - First record of the name "wombat" by John Price and James Wilson.
  • 1830 - The Tasmanian aboriginal story tells of the wombat, the great spirit Moihernee had asked hunters to leave alone.
  • 1906 - Wombats were classified as vermin.
  • 1925 - A bounty was introduced.
  • 2010 - A 59-year-old man from rural Victoria state was mauled by a wombat.

-- American writer William Jacob "Will" Cuppy
